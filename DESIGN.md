## Brand & Style
The design system embodies an aura of exclusive, high-stakes transformation. It is tailored for high-performance individuals seeking a "Transformation Mentor" who balances scientific rigor with philosophical depth.

The aesthetic is **Sophisticated Minimalism** with a **Tactile Noir** influence. By using a strict pure black base, the interface recedes to let the content feel emergent and profound. Visual interest is generated through light-interaction rather than physical objects—utilizing thin metallic strokes and deep-hued glows to signify neural pathways and biological transformation. The emotional response is one of quiet confidence, authority, and ultimate discretion.

## Colors Detail
This design system utilizes a "Void" palette, where pure black (#000000) is the absolute foundation for all sections to maintain a high-end, cinematic feel.

- **Primary (Cobalt Blue):** Used for scientific markers, data visualization, and subheadings that denote methodology and logic. (`#3f448c` / `#bfc2ff`)
- **Secondary (Deep Vinotinto):** Reserved for thematic sections involving deep emotional work, neural rewiring, and passion. Used sparingly to maintain its gravity. (`#5c1212` / `#ffb3ad`)
- **Luxury Accent (Metallic Gold):** Applied to the brand mark, primary calls to action, and structural "hairline" dividers to signify premium value. (`#faba72`)
- **Neutrals:** Grays are avoided; instead, use low-opacity versions of Cobalt or Vinotinto for subtle text states to keep the palette rich.

## Typography Detail
The typographic hierarchy creates a tension between the traditional authority of the serif and the modern efficiency of the sans-serif.

- **Headlines:** Playfair Display provides a high-contrast, editorial feel. Display sizes should use tight letter spacing for a more aggressive, premium look.
- **Body & Labels:** Hanken Grotesk is chosen for its clean, Swiss-inspired geometry. Use lighter weights (300) for large body blocks to maintain a "breathable" feel against the black background.
- **Subheadings:** Always set in Hanken Grotesk with wide tracking (0.15em) and uppercase styling to act as scientific anchors for the serif headlines.

## Layout & Spacing Detail
The layout follows a strict 8px grid system. To convey exclusivity, whitespace (or rather, "blackspace") is maximized. 

- **Grid:** A 12-column fixed grid on desktop (max-width 1440px) with 24px gutters.
- **Vertical Rhythm:** Sections are separated by large gaps (160px+) to allow the user to mentally process each stage of the transformation narrative.
- **Alignment:** Use asymmetrical layouts where headlines occupy the left 4 columns and body text occupies columns 6-10 to create a sophisticated, editorial flow.

## Elevation & Depth Detail
In an all-black environment, traditional shadows are invisible. Depth is instead created through **Luminance and Stroke**.

- **Thin Borders:** Use 0.5px or 1px "hairline" borders in Metallic Gold or low-opacity Cobalt to define containers.
- **Tonal Tiers:** Interactive surfaces use a slightly "lighter" black (e.g., #0A0A0A) or a very subtle inner glow of Vinotinto to indicate they are raised from the void.
- **Gradient Glows:** Use ultra-diffused, 5-10% opacity radial gradients of Primary/Secondary colors behind key content blocks to create a "neural aura" effect.

## Shapes Detail
The design system utilizes **Sharp (0px)** roundedness. 

Right angles communicate precision, discipline, and structural integrity. All buttons, input fields, and cards must maintain perfectly square corners. The only exception is for circular decorative elements that represent biological cells or neural nodes, which should be perfect circles.

## Components Detail
- **Primary CTA:** Sharp-edged buttons with a Metallic Gold border (1px) and centered text in Hanken Grotesk (Bold, Uppercase). Hover state fills the button with Gold and shifts text to Black.
- **Transformation Cards:** Feature 1px Cobalt Blue borders and no background fill. On hover, the border transitions to Vinotinto with a subtle internal glow.
- **Input Fields:** Minimalist "Floating Label" style. A single Gold bottom-border that glows when the field is active. No side or top borders.
- **Lists:** Bullet points are replaced with small, 4px solid Gold squares.
- **Dividers:** Use horizontal rules with a 0.5px thickness. A small diamond shape in the center of the rule can be used for section transitions.
- **Progress Markers:** For multi-step transformation journeys, use thin vertical lines that light up in Cobalt as the user progresses.
